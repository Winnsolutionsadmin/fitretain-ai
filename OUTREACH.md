## Outreach Guidelines

### Target Profile
- **Primary:** Independent gym/fitness center owners
- **Secondary:** Gym managers with P&L responsibility
- **Size:** 200-1,500 active members
- **Location:** Sacramento, Placer County, Folsom, Roseville
- **Pain Level:** Experiencing 6%+ monthly churn

### Sending Schedule
- **Email sequence:** Days 1, 4, 7, 11, 16 (5 touches over 16 days)
- **SMS follow-up:** Days 3, 9, 14 (between email touches)
- **Optimal timing:** Tuesday-Thursday 10am-2pm PT

### Personalization Variables
- `[First Name]` — prospect's first name
- `[Gym Name]` — their business name
- `[Location]` — city/neighborhood
- `[Estimated Members]` — rough member count from research
- `[Monthly Fee]` — estimated membership fee from website/research

### Success Metrics to Track
- **Open rates:** Target 35%+ (vs 21% industry average)
- **Reply rates:** Target 8%+ (vs 3% industry average)
- **Demo booking rate:** Target 3%+ of total emails sent
- **SMS response rate:** Target 15%+ (vs 6% industry average)

### Compliance Notes
- Include unsubscribe link in all emails
- Honor opt-out requests within 24 hours
- SMS opt-in required for text campaigns
- CAN-SPAM and TCPA compliant language

### A/B Testing Opportunities
- **Subject lines:** Question vs. statement vs. curiosity gap
- **Email length:** Short (150 words) vs. medium (300 words)
- **CTA placement:** Top vs. bottom vs. multiple
- **Social proof:** Specific names vs. anonymous testimonials
- **Urgency language:** Deadline-based vs. loss-aversion
